Improved Apple Timer

The Improved Apple Timer allows you to enter the correct values for which you would want your timer to go off at. It allows an easier way to set values by typing in the specific numbers for your Hours, Minutes, and Seconds. The seconds category was added to make your time be more specific if needed.
